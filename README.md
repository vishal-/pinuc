# Pinuc

Pinuc is a service-to-consumer platform that connects customers with trusted local service providers. Whether you need home repairs, tutoring, design services, or wellness treatments, Pinuc makes it easy to find, book, and pay for professional services in your area.

## What We Offer

- **Find Trusted Professionals**: Browse verified service providers in your local area
- **Easy Booking**: Schedule appointments with just a few clicks
- **Secure Payments**: Complete transactions safely through our platform
- **Wide Range of Services**: From home repair and tutoring to design and wellness services

## How It Works

1. **Search**: Find the perfect service provider for your needs
2. **Book**: Schedule your appointment with ease
3. **Pay Securely**: Complete payment safely through our platform

ServiceHub simplifies the process of finding reliable local services, giving you peace of mind and saving you time.
